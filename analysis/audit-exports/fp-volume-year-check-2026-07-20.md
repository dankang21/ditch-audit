# Verification memo — *Faith and Philosophy* volume-year mapping (2026-07-20)

Claim under check (draft §4.1, OpenAlex backfill of the Crossref deposit gap 2022–2023):
issue years derived from volume numbers via year = 1983 + volume.

Findings (publisher/repository records):

1. The mapping year = 1983 + volume is **confirmed for volumes 38–40** (= 2021–2023),
   the volumes actually used by the backfill.
2. *Faith and Philosophy* published **no volume with cover year 2024**. The journal
   skipped from volume 40 (2023) to volume 41 (2025), re-syncing volume numbers to
   calendar years; from volume 41 onward the mapping is year = 1984 + volume.
   Evidence: the Asbury repository lists volume 41 issues under 2025
   (place.asburyseminary.edu/faithandphilosophy/vol41/iss1/), and pdcnet volume-41 PDFs
   carry the masthead "Vol. 41 No. 2 April 2025".
3. **Corpus impact: none.** No volume-41 item was harvested; the venue's 2024 cell is
   empty at the source (unpublished), not unharvested. The backfill's 65 items all lie
   in volumes 38–40, where the registered mapping is exact.

Disposition: the draft's earlier parenthetical ("volume 41, i.e., 2024, is absent from
the source itself — unpublished, not unharvested") inferred the volume-year identity of
the missing volume from the formula rather than the record; corrected at v0.4 (§4.1) to
state the verified facts above. Inventoried in the v0.4 revision note, item (3).
